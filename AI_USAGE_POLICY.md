# AI Usage Policy

## Overview

Defines acceptable use of AI-generated content in BaoProject.

## Allowed

- Code improvements, bug fixes, and documentation.
- Suggestions that follow repository style.
- Non-sensitive, public domain, or appropriately licensed content.

## Not allowed

- Generating copyrighted material without permission.
- Adding unsafe, harmful, or malicious content.
- Sharing credentials or secrets.
- Making speculative architectural changes without context.

## Review

- Human review is required for all significant AI contributions.
- Verify correctness and relevance before merging.
